Based on the sources, here are the **Top 10 English keywords with Chinese definitions** that represent the core concepts of the book:

1.  **Information System (資訊系統):** 一組相互關聯的組件，透過收集、處理、存儲和分發資訊，以支持組織的決策、協調與控制。
2.  **Hardware (硬體):** 資訊系統中切實存在的物理部分，即您可以觸摸到的設備，如電腦、鍵盤和硬碟。
3.  **Software (軟體):** 告訴硬體該做什麼的一組指令，通常分為操作系統（如 Windows）和應用軟體（如 Excel）兩大類。
4.  **Data (數據/資料):** 原始事實的集合，其本身可能缺乏背景資訊，但經過加工處理後可轉化為對決策有用的資訊或知識。
5.  **Process (流程):** 為了實現特定目標或業務成果而採取的一系列任務或步驟。
6.  **Competitive Advantage (競爭優勢):** 當一家公司能夠持續獲得高於行業平均水平的利潤時，即具備競爭優勢，通常透過成本優勢或差異化優勢達成。
7.  **Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP, 企業資源規劃):** 一套帶有集中式資料庫的軟體應用程式，用於整合並運行一個組織內的所有業務模組，如財務、生產和銷售。
8.  **Cloud Computing (雲端運算):** 位於網際網路上的應用程式、服務和數據存儲資源，用戶無需在本地安裝即可隨時存取。
9.  **Globalization (全球化):** 世界各國之間商品、服務和文化的整合，資訊技術（如個人電腦與光纖網路）是推動此趨勢的核心力量。
10. **Digital Divide (數位落差):** 能夠使用全球化網路的人與無法使用的人之間的鴻溝，其不平等可能發生在國家、地區或社群之間。

**Analogy for Understanding an Information System**
To better understand these keywords, think of an **Information System** as a **Professional Kitchen**. The **Hardware** is the physical equipment (stoves, knives); the **Software** is the recipes that tell you how to use the equipment; the **Data** is the raw ingredients; the **People** are the chefs and staff; and the **Process** is the organized workflow from taking an order to serving the dish. When these elements work together perfectly, the restaurant gains a **Competitive Advantage** over others.
Here are the top 10 keywords from the sources:

1.  **Information System (資訊系統):** Interrelated components that collect and process data to support organizational decision-making.
2.  **Hardware (硬體):** Tangible, physical components of a system, such as computers and keyboards.
3.  **Software (軟體):** Intangible sets of instructions that tell the hardware what to do.
4.  **Data (數據):** Raw facts that, when organized into databases, provide powerful business insights.
5.  **People (人員):** Individuals involved in designing, managing, and using information systems.
6.  **Process (流程):** A series of steps taken to achieve a desired outcome or goal.
7.  **Competitive Advantage (競爭優勢):** Sustaining profits that exceed the industry average through cost leadership or differentiation.
8.  **ERP (企業資源規劃):** A software application with a centralized database used to run an entire company.
9.  **Globalization (全球化):** The integration of goods, services, and culture among nations via technology.
10. **Digital Divide (數位落差):** The separation between those who have access to global networks and those who do not.
